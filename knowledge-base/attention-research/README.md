# Attention Research

**Overview**: Scientific understanding of attention as a finite cognitive resource, its depletion, restoration, and optimization.

## Core Principle

> "Your life is the sum of what you pay attention to" - Winifred Gallagher

Attention is not unlimited. Directed attention depletes with use, requires restoration, and suffers from residue when switching contexts. Understanding attention's constraints is fundamental to knowledge work performance.

## Two Types of Attention

| Type | Characteristics | Resource Cost |
|------|----------------|---------------|
| **Directed Attention** | Voluntary, effortful, focused | Depletes with use |
| **Involuntary Attention** | Captured by inherently interesting stimuli | Doesn't deplete |

## Quick Start

1. **Time-block deep work**: Protect 90-120 minute blocks for cognitively demanding tasks
2. **Eliminate context switches**: Single-task during deep work periods
3. **Create Ready-to-Resume plans**: Document where you are + next steps when interrupted
4. **Schedule restoration**: Regular exposure to nature or "soft fascination" environments
5. **Track attention span**: Notice when focus degrades, adjust session length
